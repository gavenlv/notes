## 5.1 考试准备策略 | Exam Preparation Strategies

### 学习计划制定 | Study Plan Development

**中文策略**：
- **长期规划**：提前6-9个月开始准备
- **分阶段学习**：基础学习→强化练习→模拟考试
- **时间分配**：根据权重分配学习时间
- **定期评估**：每月评估学习进度和效果

**English Strategies**:
- **Long-term Planning**: Start preparation 6-9 months in advance
- **Phased Learning**: Basic study → Intensive practice → Mock exams
- **Time Allocation**: Allocate study time based on weightings
- **Regular Assessment**: Monthly evaluation of progress and effectiveness

### 学习方法优化 | Learning Method Optimization

**中文技巧**：
- **主动学习**：做笔记、总结、教授他人
- **间隔重复**：定期复习已学内容
- **错题分析**：深入分析错误原因
- **模拟考试**：全真模拟考试环境

**English Techniques**:
- **Active Learning**: Note-taking, summarizing, teaching others
- **Spaced Repetition**: Regular review of learned content
- **Error Analysis**: Deep analysis of mistake causes
- **Mock Exams**: Full simulation of exam environment

## 5.2 考试技巧 | Exam Techniques

### 时间管理 | Time Management

**中文时间分配**：
- **CFA一级**：180题/3小时 = 1分钟/题
- **CFA二级**：88项目集/6小时 = 4分钟/题
- **CFA三级**：构建式回答需合理分配时间
- **检查时间**：预留10-15分钟检查答案

**English Time Allocation**:
- **Level 1**: 180 questions/3 hours = 1 minute/question
- **Level 2**: 88 item sets/6 hours = 4 minutes/question
- **Level 3**: Reasonable time allocation for constructed response
- **Review Time**: Reserve 10-15 minutes for answer review

### 答题策略 | Answering Strategies

**中文技巧**：
- **先易后难**：先做熟悉的题目
- **标记难题**：暂时跳过，最后处理
- **排除法**：排除明显错误的选项
- **计算题检查**：重新计算验证答案

**English Techniques**:
- **Easy First**: Start with familiar questions
- **Flag Difficult**: Skip temporarily, handle later
- **Elimination**: Eliminate obviously wrong choices
- **Calculation Check**: Recalculate to verify answers

## 5.3 心理准备 | Psychological Preparation

**English Coping Methods**:
- **Thorough Preparation**: Confidence comes from adequate preparation
- **Relaxation Techniques**: Deep breathing, meditation, muscle relaxation
- **Positive Mindset**: Believe in your ability to succeed
- **Stress Relief**: Appropriate exercise, social interaction

### 考试当天准备 | Exam Day Preparation

**中文清单**：
- **证件准备**：护照、准考证
- **物品准备**：计算器、铅笔、橡皮
- **时间安排**：提前到达考场

**English Checklist**:
