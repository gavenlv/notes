## 一级练习题 | Level 1 Practice Questions

### 数量方法 | Quantitative Methods

**题目1**：如果年利率为8%，每季度复利一次，那么有效年利率是多少？

**Question 1**: If the annual interest rate is 8%, compounded quarterly, what is the effective annual rate?

**选项** | **Options**:
A) 8.00%
B) 8.24%
C) 8.30%
D) 8.50%

**答案**：B) 8.24%
**Answer**: B) 8.24%

**解析**：有效年利率 = (1 + 0.08/4)^4 - 1 = (1.02)^4 - 1 = 1.0824 - 1 = 0.0824 = 8.24%
**Explanation**: EAR = (1 + 0.08/4)^4 - 1 = (1.02)^4 - 1 = 1.0824 - 1 = 0.0824 = 8.24%

**考试技巧**：
- **计算器操作**：TI BA II Plus: 8 ÷ 4 = 2, 1 + 2% = 1.02, y^x 4 = 1.0824, -1 = 0.0824
- **公式记忆**：EAR = (1 + r/n)^n - 1，其中r为名义年利率，n为复利次数
- **常见错误**：忘记减1，直接使用(1 + r/n)^n作为结果

**Exam Techniques**:
- **Calculator**: TI BA II Plus: 8 ÷ 4 = 2, 1 + 2% = 1.02, y^x 4 = 1.0824, -1 = 0.0824
- **Formula**: EAR = (1 + r/n)^n - 1, where r is nominal rate, n is compounding frequency
- **Common errors**: Forgetting to subtract 1, using (1 + r/n)^n as result

**题目2**：一个投资组合的预期收益为12%，标准差为20%，无风险利率为4%。该组合的夏普比率是多少？

**Question 2**: A portfolio has an expected return of 12%, standard deviation of 20%, and the risk-free rate is 4%. What is the Sharpe ratio?

**选项** | **Options**:
A) 0.2
B) 0.4
C) 0.6
D) 0.8

**答案**：B) 0.4
**Answer**: B) 0.4

**解析**：夏普比率 = (12% - 4%) / 20% = 8% / 20% = 0.4
**Explanation**: Sharpe ratio = (12% - 4%) / 20% = 8% / 20% = 0.4

### 财务报表分析 | Financial Statement Analysis

**题目3**：某公司净利润为100万元，总资产为500万元，股东权益为200万元。该公司的ROA和ROE分别是多少？

**Question 3**: A company has net income of 1 million, total assets of 5 million, and shareholders' equity of 2 million. What are its ROA and ROE?

**选项** | **Options**:
A) ROA=10%, ROE=20%
B) ROA=20%, ROE=50%
C) ROA=15%, ROE=30%
D) ROA=25%, ROE=40%

**答案**：B) ROA=20%, ROE=50%
**Answer**: B) ROA=20%, ROE=50%

**解析**：ROA = 净利润/总资产 = 100/500 = 20%；ROE = 净利润/股东权益 = 100/200 = 50%
**Explanation**: ROA = Net income/Total assets = 1/5 = 20%；ROE = Net income/Equity = 1/2 = 50%

**Question 4**: According to IFRS, how should R&D expenditures be treated?

## 二级练习题 | Level 2 Practice Questions

### 权益估值 | Equity Valuation

**题目5**：某公司预计下一年股利为2元，股利增长率为5%，要求回报率为10%。根据股利贴现模型，该股票的内在价值是多少？

**Question 5**: A company expects next year's dividend to be $2, dividend growth rate is 5%, required return is 10%. According to the dividend discount model, what is the intrinsic value?

**选项** | **Options**:
A) $20
B) $30
C) $40
D) $50

**答案**：C) $40
**Answer**: C) $40

**解析**：P0 = D1 / (r - g) = 2 / (0.10 - 0.05) = 2 / 0.05 = $40
**Explanation**: P0 = D1 / (r - g) = 2 / (0.10 - 0.05) = 2 / 0.05 = $40

**题目6**：在相对估值中，哪个倍数最适合比较不同资本结构的公司？

**Question 6**: In relative valuation, which multiple is most suitable for comparing companies with different capital structures?

**选项** | **Options**:
A) P/E ratio
B) P/B ratio
C) EV/EBITDA
D) P/S ratio

**答案**：C) EV/EBITDA
**Answer**: C) EV/EBITDA

**解析**：EV/EBITDA不受资本结构影响，更适合比较不同杠杆水平的公司
**Explanation**: EV/EBITDA is not affected by capital structure, more suitable for comparing companies with different leverage levels

## 三级练习题 | Level 3 Practice Questions

### 资产配置 | Asset Allocation

**题目8**：一个投资者风险承受能力中等，投资期限10年，主要目标是资本增值。请推荐一个合适的资产配置方案。

**Question 8**: An investor has medium risk tolerance, 10-year investment horizon, primary goal is capital appreciation. Recommend a suitable asset allocation.

**推荐方案** | **Recommended Allocation**:
- **股票**：60%
- **债券**：30%
- **现金**：5%
- **另类投资**：5%

**理由**：中等风险承受能力适合平衡型配置，10年投资期限允许承担一定风险以实现资本增值
**Rationale**: Medium risk tolerance suits balanced allocation, 10-year horizon allows taking some risk for capital appreciation

**题目9**：解释行为金融中的"损失厌恶"概念及其对投资决策的影响。

**Question 9**: Explain the concept of "loss aversion" in behavioral finance and its impact on investment decisions.

**答案要点** | **Key Points**:
- **定义**：投资者对损失的感受强于同等金额的收益
- **影响**：导致过早卖出盈利股票，持有亏损股票过久
- **对策**：建立系统性投资策略，避免情绪化决策

**Answer**: Loss aversion means investors feel losses more strongly than gains of equal amount. This leads to selling winners too early and holding losers too long. The solution is to establish systematic investment strategies to avoid emotional decisions.

## 职业道德 | Ethical Standards

**题目10**：你发现同事在客户报告中使用了未经核实的数据。根据CFA职业道德准则，你应该怎么做？

**Question 10**: You discover a colleague used unverified data in a client report. According to CFA ethical standards, what should you do?

**正确做法** | **Correct Action**:
1. 首先与同事私下沟通，了解情况
2. 如果问题未解决，向主管或合规部门报告
3. 确保客户利益不受损害
4. 遵循公司内部报告程序

**依据**：CFA准则要求维护专业诚信和保护客户利益
**Basis**: CFA standards require maintaining professional integrity and protecting client interests
