## 描述性统计 (Descriptive Statistics)

### 中文解释
描述性统计是用来总结和描述数据集特征的方法。在投资分析中，我们经常需要分析股票收益率、市场指数等数据的特征。

#### 主要概念：
1. **集中趋势度量**：
   - 算术平均数（Arithmetic Mean）
   - 中位数（Median）
   - 众数（Mode）

2. **离散程度度量**：
   - 极差（Range）
   - 方差（Variance）
   - 标准差（Standard Deviation）
   - 四分位距（Interquartile Range）

3. **偏度与峰度**：
   - 偏度（Skewness）：衡量分布的对称性
   - 峰度（Kurtosis）：衡量分布的尖峭程度

### English Explanation
Descriptive statistics are methods used to summarize and describe the characteristics of a dataset. In investment analysis, we often need to analyze characteristics of data such as stock returns and market indices.

#### Key Concepts:
1. **Measures of Central Tendency**:
   - Arithmetic Mean
   - Median
   - Mode

2. **Measures of Dispersion**:
   - Range
   - Variance
   - Standard Deviation
   - Interquartile Range

3. **Skewness and Kurtosis**:
   - Skewness: Measures the symmetry of a distribution
   - Kurtosis: Measures the peakedness of a distribution

## 概率论基础 (Probability Fundamentals)

### 中文解释
概率论是定量分析的核心，帮助我们理解和量化不确定性。

#### 关键概念：
1. **概率的基本性质**：
   - 概率值介于0和1之间
   - 所有可能事件的概率之和等于1

2. **联合概率、边缘概率和条件概率**：
   - 联合概率：两个事件同时发生的概率
   - 边缘概率：单个事件发生的概率
   - 条件概率：在某个条件下事件发生的概率

3. **贝叶斯公式**：
   - 用于更新先验概率得到后验概率
   - 在投资决策中有重要应用

### English Explanation
Probability theory is the core of quantitative analysis, helping us understand and quantify uncertainty.

#### Key Concepts:
1. **Basic Properties of Probability**:
   - Probability values range between 0 and 1
   - The sum of probabilities of all possible events equals 1

2. **Joint, Marginal, and Conditional Probability**:
   - Joint Probability: Probability of two events occurring simultaneously
   - Marginal Probability: Probability of a single event occurring
   - Conditional Probability: Probability of an event occurring given a condition

3. **Bayes' Formula**:
   - Used to update prior probabilities to obtain posterior probabilities
   - Has important applications in investment decision-making

## 常见概率分布 (Common Probability Distributions)

### 中文解释
在金融分析中，我们需要了解几种重要的概率分布及其应用。

#### 1. 正态分布 (Normal Distribution)
- 特征：钟形曲线，对称分布
- 应用：许多金融变量近似服从正态分布

#### 2. 对数正态分布 (Lognormal Distribution)
- 特征：右偏分布，只能取正值
- 应用：股票价格通常服从对数正态分布

#### 3. 学生t分布 (Student's t-Distribution)
- 特征：比正态分布有更厚的尾部
- 应用：小样本情况下估计总体均值

#### 4. 卡方分布 (Chi-Square Distribution)
- 特征：右偏分布，用于检验方差
- 应用：假设检验中的方差检验

#### 5. F分布 (F-Distribution)
- 特征：右偏分布，用于比较两个方差
- 应用：回归分析中的方差分析

### English Explanation
In financial analysis, we need to understand several important probability distributions and their applications.

#### 1. Normal Distribution
- Characteristics: Bell-shaped curve, symmetric distribution
- Applications: Many financial variables approximately follow a normal distribution

#### 2. Lognormal Distribution
- Characteristics: Right-skewed distribution, takes only positive values
- Applications: Stock prices typically follow a lognormal distribution

#### 3. Student's t-Distribution
- Characteristics: Thicker tails than normal distribution
- Applications: Estimating population mean with small samples

#### 4. Chi-Square Distribution
- Characteristics: Right-skewed distribution, used to test variance
- Applications: Variance testing in hypothesis testing

#### 5. F-Distribution
- Characteristics: Right-skewed distribution, used to compare two variances
- Applications: Analysis of variance in regression analysis

## 抽样和估计 (Sampling and Estimation)

### 中文解释
抽样是从总体中选取一部分个体进行研究的过程，而估计则是利用样本数据推断总体参数。

#### 关键概念：
1. **抽样方法**：
   - 简单随机抽样
   - 分层抽样
   - 系统抽样
   - 整群抽样

2. **估计量的性质**：
   - 无偏性：估计量的期望值等于真实参数
   - 有效性：方差较小的估计量更有效
   - 一致性：样本容量增大时估计量趋近于真实参数

3. **置信区间**：
   - 用于表示参数估计的不确定性
   - 置信水平越高，区间越宽

### English Explanation
Sampling is the process of selecting a subset of individuals from a population for study, while estimation involves inferring population parameters using sample data.

#### Key Concepts:
1. **Sampling Methods**:
   - Simple Random Sampling
   - Stratified Sampling
   - Systematic Sampling
   - Cluster Sampling

2. **Properties of Estimators**:
   - Unbiasedness: The expected value of the estimator equals the true parameter
   - Efficiency: An estimator with smaller variance is more efficient
   - Consistency: As sample size increases, the estimator approaches the true parameter

3. **Confidence Intervals**:
   - Used to express uncertainty in parameter estimates
   - Higher confidence levels result in wider intervals

## 假设检验 (Hypothesis Testing)

### 中文解释
假设检验是一种统计推断方法，用于判断关于总体参数的假设是否成立。

#### 基本步骤：
1. 建立原假设(H₀)和备择假设(H₁)
2. 选择显著性水平(通常为5%)
3. 计算检验统计量
4. 确定临界值或p值
5. 做出统计决策

#### 两类错误：
- 第一类错误：原假设为真时拒绝原假设（弃真错误）
- 第二类错误：原假设为假时接受原假设（取伪错误）

### English Explanation
Hypothesis testing is a statistical inference method used to determine whether hypotheses about population parameters are valid.

#### Basic Steps:
1. Establish null hypothesis (H₀) and alternative hypothesis (H₁)
2. Choose significance level (typically 5%)
3. Calculate test statistic
4. Determine critical value or p-value
5. Make statistical decision

#### Two Types of Errors:
- Type I Error: Rejecting a true null hypothesis (false positive)
- Type II Error: Accepting a false null hypothesis (false negative)

## 实际应用案例 (Practical Application Cases)

### 案例1：风险评估 (Case 1: Risk Assessment)

#### 中文情景
某投资组合在过去5年的年收益率分别为：8%, 12%, -5%, 15%, 6%。请计算该投资组合的平均收益率和标准差。

#### 解答过程
1. 平均收益率 = (8% + 12% + (-5%) + 15% + 6%) / 5 = 7.2%
2. 方差 = [(8%-7.2%)² + (12%-7.2%)² + (-5%-7.2%)² + (15%-7.2%)² + (6%-7.2%)²] / 5 = 59.36
3. 标准差 = √59.36 ≈ 7.7%

#### 英文 Scenario
A portfolio has generated annual returns of 8%, 12%, -5%, 15%, and 6% over the past 5 years. Calculate the average return and standard deviation of this portfolio.

#### Solution Process
1. Average return = (8% + 12% + (-5%) + 15% + 6%) / 5 = 7.2%
2. Variance = [(8%-7.2%)² + (12%-7.2%)² + (-5%-7.2%)² + (15%-7.2%)² + (6%-7.2%)²] / 5 = 59.36
3. Standard deviation = √59.36 ≈ 7.7%
