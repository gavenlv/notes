# CFA一级考试完整学习指南

## 简介 (Introduction)

CFA（Chartered Financial Analyst）是全球投资分析领域最权威、最专业的资格认证之一。CFA课程涵盖了投资管理、财务分析、资产评估等领域的广泛知识。

The CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) is one of the most authoritative and professional qualifications in the global investment analysis field. The CFA curriculum covers extensive knowledge in investment management, financial analysis, asset valuation, and related areas.

## 学习目标 (Learning Objectives)

本教程旨在为零基础学员提供完整的CFA一级学习路径，帮助学员：
- 理解CFA一级考试的所有核心知识点
- 掌握投资分析和资产管理的基本技能
- 通过实际案例加深对概念的理解
- 为CFA一级考试做好充分准备

This tutorial aims to provide a complete CFA Level I learning path for beginners, helping students:
- Understand all core knowledge points of the CFA Level I exam
- Master basic skills in investment analysis and asset management
- Deepen understanding of concepts through practical examples
- Prepare thoroughly for the CFA Level I exam

4. 定期复习，巩固知识点
