## 资本预算 (Capital Budgeting)

### 净现值法 (Net Present Value, NPV)

#### 中文解释
净现值法是评估投资项目最常用的方法之一。NPV是项目未来现金流的现值减去初始投资额。

计算公式：
**NPV = Σ [CFt / (1 + r)^t] - C0**

其中：
- CFt = 第t期的现金流
- r = 折现率（通常是项目的资本成本）
- t = 时间期数
- C0 = 初始投资额

决策规则：
- 如果NPV > 0，接受该项目
- 如果NPV < 0，拒绝该项目
- 如果NPV = 0，项目刚好达到要求回报率

#### 英文 Explanation
Net Present Value (NPV) is one of the most commonly used methods for evaluating investment projects. NPV is the present value of a project's future cash flows minus the initial investment.

Calculation formula:
**NPV = Σ [CFt / (1 + r)^t] - C0**

Where:
- CFt = Cash flow in period t
- r = Discount rate (usually the project's cost of capital)
- t = Time period
- C0 = Initial investment

Decision rule:
- If NPV > 0, accept the project
- If NPV < 0, reject the project
- If NPV = 0, the project just meets the required rate of return

### 内部收益率法 (Internal Rate of Return, IRR)

#### 中文解释
内部收益率是使项目净现值等于零的折现率。换句话说，IRR是项目预期能够实现的回报率。

计算原理：
**0 = Σ [CFt / (1 + IRR)^t] - C0**

决策规则：
- 如果IRR > 要求回报率，接受该项目
- 如果IRR < 要求回报率，拒绝该项目

IRR方法的优点：
1. 易于理解，表示为百分比回报率
2. 考虑了资金的时间价值
3. 考虑了项目整个生命周期的现金流

IRR方法的缺点：
1. 可能存在多个解或无解
2. 在互斥项目选择中可能产生误导
3. 假设现金流以IRR进行再投资，这可能不现实

#### 英文 Explanation
Internal Rate of Return (IRR) is the discount rate that makes the project's net present value equal to zero. In other words, IRR is the expected rate of return that the project can achieve.

Calculation principle:
**0 = Σ [CFt / (1 + IRR)^t] - C0**

Decision rule:
- If IRR > required rate of return, accept the project
- If IRR < required rate of return, reject the project

Advantages of IRR method:
1. Easy to understand, expressed as a percentage return
2. Considers the time value of money
3. Considers cash flows throughout the project's life cycle

Disadvantages of IRR method:
1. May have multiple solutions or no solution
2. May be misleading in mutually exclusive project selection
3. Assumes cash flows are reinvested at the IRR, which may be unrealistic

### 投资回收期法 (Payback Period)

#### 中文解释
投资回收期是指收回初始投资所需的时间长度。这是最简单的投资项目评估方法之一。

计算方法：
**投资回收期 = 累计现金流首次转正的年份 - 1 + (上一年度累计负现金流绝对值 / 当年度现金流)**

决策规则：
- 如果投资回收期 < 预定年限，接受该项目
- 如果投资回收期 > 预定年限，拒绝该项目

优点：
1. 计算简单，易于理解
2. 强调流动性，适用于资金紧张的企业
3. 能够快速筛选明显不合适的项目

缺点：
1. 忽略了资金的时间价值
2. 忽略了回收期后的现金流
3. 不能提供项目整体盈利性的完整信息

#### 英文 Explanation
Payback period is the length of time required to recover the initial investment. This is one of the simplest methods for evaluating investment projects.

Calculation method:
**Payback Period = Year when cumulative cash flow first turns positive - 1 + (Absolute value of cumulative negative cash flow in previous year / Cash flow in current year)**

Decision rule:
- If payback period < predetermined period, accept the project
- If payback period > predetermined period, reject the project

Advantages:
1. Simple calculation, easy to understand
2. Emphasizes liquidity, suitable for companies with tight funds
3. Can quickly screen obviously unsuitable projects

Disadvantages:
1. Ignores the time value of money
2. Ignores cash flows after the payback period
3. Cannot provide complete information on the project's overall profitability

## 资本成本 (Cost of Capital)

### 加权平均资本成本 (Weighted Average Cost of Capital, WACC)

#### 中文解释
加权平均资本成本是企业各种资本来源成本的加权平均，反映了企业为筹集资金而需要支付的平均成本。

计算公式：
**WACC = (E/V) × Re + (D/V) × Rd × (1 - Tc)**

其中：
- E = 股权市场价值
- D = 债务市场价值
- V = E + D (企业总价值)
- Re = 股权成本
- Rd = 债务成本
- Tc = 企业所得税税率

WACC的应用：
1. 作为投资项目评估的折现率
2. 用于企业价值评估
3. 作为业绩评价的基准

#### 英文 Explanation
Weighted Average Cost of Capital (WACC) is the weighted average of the costs of various sources of capital, reflecting the average cost a company needs to pay to raise funds.

Calculation formula:
**WACC = (E/V) × Re + (D/V) × Rd × (1 - Tc)**

Where:
- E = Market value of equity
- D = Market value of debt
- V = E + D (Total firm value)
- Re = Cost of equity
- Rd = Cost of debt
- Tc = Corporate tax rate

Applications of WACC:
1. As the discount rate for investment project evaluation
2. For company valuation
3. As a benchmark for performance evaluation

### 股权成本 (Cost of Equity)

#### 中文解释
股权成本是股东要求的最低回报率，代表了投资者对股权投资所要求的补偿。

资本资产定价模型(CAPM)计算股权成本：
**Re = Rf + β × (Rm - Rf)**

其中：
- Re = 股权成本
- Rf = 无风险利率
- β = 股票的贝塔系数
- Rm = 市场预期回报率
- (Rm - Rf) = 市场风险溢价

股权成本估算的其他方法：
1. 股利折现模型(DDM)
2. 债券收益率加风险溢价法

#### 英文 Explanation
Cost of equity is the minimum rate of return required by shareholders, representing the compensation investors demand for equity investment.

Capital Asset Pricing Model (CAPM) calculates cost of equity:
**Re = Rf + β × (Rm - Rf)**

Where:
- Re = Cost of equity
- Rf = Risk-free rate
- β = Stock's beta coefficient
- Rm = Expected market return
- (Rm - Rf) = Market risk premium

Other methods for estimating cost of equity:
1. Dividend Discount Model (DDM)
2. Bond yield plus risk premium approach

## 资本结构 (Capital Structure)

### 莫迪利亚尼-米勒定理 (Modigliani-Miller Theorem)

#### 中文解释
莫迪利亚尼-米勒定理(MM定理)是现代公司金融理论的基石，提出了关于资本结构与企业价值关系的重要观点。

MM第一定理(无税情况下)：
在完美市场假设下，企业的价值与其资本结构无关。即无论企业采用何种债务与股权比例，其总价值保持不变。

MM第二定理(无税情况下)：
股权成本随着财务杠杆的增加而线性增加，其斜率等于(市场回报率 - 无风险利率)。

考虑税收的MM定理：
当引入企业所得税时，由于债务利息可以抵税，企业价值会随着债务比例的增加而增加。

现实世界的修正：
在现实中，除了税收因素外，还有破产成本、代理成本等因素会影响最优资本结构的选择。

#### 英文 Explanation
The Modigliani-Miller theorem (MM theorem) is the cornerstone of modern corporate finance theory, presenting important insights about the relationship between capital structure and firm value.

MM Proposition I (without taxes):
Under perfect market assumptions, a firm's value is independent of its capital structure. That is, regardless of the debt-to-equity ratio a company adopts, its total value remains unchanged.

MM Proposition II (without taxes):
The cost of equity increases linearly with financial leverage, with a slope equal to (market return - risk-free rate).

MM theorem with taxes:
When corporate income tax is introduced, due to the tax deductibility of interest payments, firm value increases with the debt ratio.

Real-world modifications:
In reality, besides tax factors, bankruptcy costs, agency costs, and other factors affect the choice of optimal capital structure.

### 最优资本结构 (Optimal Capital Structure)

#### 中文解释
最优资本结构是指能使企业价值最大化或加权平均资本成本最小化的债务与股权组合。

影响因素：
1. 税收优势：债务利息的税盾效应
2. 财务困境成本：过度负债可能导致的破产成本
3. 代理成本：股东与债权人之间的利益冲突
4. 行业特征：不同行业的资本结构特点不同
5. 公司规模：大公司通常能获得更低的融资成本
6. 盈利能力：盈利能力强的公司对债务依赖较小

确定最优资本结构的方法：
1. 比较不同资本结构下的WACC
2. 分析不同资本结构对企业价值的影响
3. 考虑行业平均水平和最佳实践

#### 英文 Explanation
Optimal capital structure refers to the debt-to-equity combination that maximizes firm value or minimizes weighted average cost of capital.

Influencing factors:
1. Tax advantages: Tax shield effect of interest payments
2. Financial distress costs: Bankruptcy costs that may result from excessive debt
3. Agency costs: Conflicts of interest between shareholders and creditors
4. Industry characteristics: Different industries have different capital structure characteristics
5. Company size: Larger companies typically can obtain lower financing costs
6. Profitability: Companies with strong profitability have less dependence on debt

Methods for determining optimal capital structure:
1. Compare WACC under different capital structures
2. Analyze the impact of different capital structures on firm value
3. Consider industry averages and best practices

## 股利政策 (Dividend Policy)

### 股利政策理论 (Dividend Policy Theories)

#### 中文解释
股利政策是指公司决定将多少利润分配给股东以及以何种方式分配的策略。

主要股利政策理论：

1. **股利无关论(MM理论)**：
   在完美市场假设下，股利政策不影响公司价值。投资者可以通过自制股利来实现相同的现金流模式。

2. **一鸟在手理论**：
   投资者偏好当前股利而非未来的资本增值，因为当前股利更加确定。

3. **税收差别理论**：
   由于资本利得税率通常低于股利税率，投资者更偏好资本利得。

4. **信号传递理论**：
   股利政策变化可以向市场传递公司未来前景的信息。

#### 英文 Explanation
Dividend policy refers to a company's strategy for deciding how much profit to distribute to shareholders and in what form.

Major dividend policy theories:

1. **Dividend Irrelevance Theory (MM Theory)**:
   Under perfect market assumptions, dividend policy does not affect company value. Investors can achieve the same cash flow pattern through homemade dividends.

2. **Bird-in-the-Hand Theory**:
   Investors prefer current dividends over future capital appreciation because current dividends are more certain.

3. **Tax Preference Theory**:
   Since capital gains tax rates are typically lower than dividend tax rates, investors prefer capital gains.

4. **Signaling Theory**:
   Changes in dividend policy can signal information about a company's future prospects to the market.

### 股利政策类型 (Types of Dividend Policies)

#### 中文解释
常见的股利政策类型包括：

1. **稳定股利政策**：
   公司每年支付相对稳定的股利，即使盈利有所波动。

2. **固定股利支付率政策**：
   公司按固定比例分配净利润作为股利。

3. **剩余股利政策**：
   公司首先满足投资需要，然后将剩余利润作为股利分配。

4. **低正常股利加额外股利政策**：
   公司支付较低的正常股利，在盈利较好的年份支付额外股利。

股利支付的形式：
1. 现金股利
2. 股票股利
3. 股票回购

#### 英文 Explanation
Common types of dividend policies include:

1. **Stable Dividend Policy**:
   The company pays relatively stable dividends each year, even if earnings fluctuate.

2. **Fixed Dividend Payout Ratio Policy**:
   The company distributes a fixed proportion of net profits as dividends.

3. **Residual Dividend Policy**:
   The company first satisfies investment needs, then distributes remaining profits as dividends.

4. **Low Regular Dividend Plus Extras Policy**:
   The company pays low regular dividends and additional dividends in years with good earnings.

Forms of dividend payment:
1. Cash dividends
2. Stock dividends
3. Stock repurchases

## 实际应用案例 (Practical Application Cases)

### 案例1：NPV与IRR决策冲突 (Case 1: NPV vs IRR Decision Conflict)

#### 中文情景
某公司正在考虑两个互斥项目，相关数据如下：

| 项目 | 初始投资 | 第1年现金流 | 第2年现金流 | 第3年现金流 |
|------|----------|-------------|-------------|-------------|
| A    | -1000    | 800         | 600         | 200         |
| B    | -1000    | 200         | 600         | 1200        |

假设折现率为10%，请分别计算两个项目的NPV和IRR，并分析可能出现的决策冲突。

#### 解答过程
项目A：
- NPV = -1000 + 800/(1.1) + 600/(1.1²) + 200/(1.1³) = -1000 + 727.27 + 495.87 + 150.26 = 373.40
- IRR计算：0 = -1000 + 800/(1+IRR) + 600/(1+IRR)² + 200/(1+IRR)³，解得IRR ≈ 35.7%

项目B：
- NPV = -1000 + 200/(1.1) + 600/(1.1²) + 1200/(1.1³) = -1000 + 181.82 + 495.87 + 901.58 = 579.27
- IRR计算：0 = -1000 + 200/(1+IRR) + 600/(1+IRR)² + 1200/(1+IRR)³，解得IRR ≈ 24.2%

根据NPV法则应选择项目B，根据IRR法则应选择项目A，出现决策冲突。

冲突原因分析：
1. 项目规模相同但现金流时间分布不同
2. 项目A早期现金流较大，项目B后期现金流较大
3. 这种情况下应以NPV为准，因为它直接反映了股东财富的增加

#### 英文 Scenario
A company is considering two mutually exclusive projects with the following data:

| Project | Initial Investment | Year 1 Cash Flow | Year 2 Cash Flow | Year 3 Cash Flow |
|---------|-------------------|------------------|------------------|------------------|
| A       | -1000             | 800              | 600              | 200              |
| B       | -1000             | 200              | 600              | 1200             |

Assuming a discount rate of 10%, please calculate the NPV and IRR for both projects and analyze potential decision conflicts.

#### Solution Process
Project A:
- NPV = -1000 + 800/(1.1) + 600/(1.1²) + 200/(1.1³) = -1000 + 727.27 + 495.87 + 150.26 = 373.40
- IRR calculation: 0 = -1000 + 800/(1+IRR) + 600/(1+IRR)² + 200/(1+IRR)³, solving gives IRR ≈ 35.7%

Project B:
- NPV = -1000 + 200/(1.1) + 600/(1.1²) + 1200/(1.1³) = -1000 + 181.82 + 495.87 + 901.58 = 579.27
- IRR calculation: 0 = -1000 + 200/(1+IRR) + 600/(1+IRR)² + 1200/(1+IRR)³, solving gives IRR ≈ 24.2%

According to the NPV rule, Project B should be selected; according to the IRR rule, Project A should be selected, resulting in a decision conflict.

Conflict analysis:
1. Projects have the same scale but different cash flow timing distributions
2. Project A has larger early cash flows, while Project B has larger late cash flows
3. In this situation, NPV should be used as the criterion since it directly reflects the increase in shareholder wealth
