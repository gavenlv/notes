# 第2部分：CFA一级核心知识模块 | Part 2: CFA Level 1 Core Knowledge Modules

## 2.1 职业道德标准 | Ethical and Professional Standards (15-20%)

### 职业道德准则 | Code of Ethics

**中文核心内容**：
- **七大准则**：专业性、诚信正直、客户责任、雇主责任、投资分析、利益冲突、会员责任
- **职业行为标准**：具体的行为规范和要求
- **全球投资业绩标准**：业绩报告的标准化要求

**English Core Content**:
- **Seven Standards**: Professionalism, Integrity, Duties to Clients, Duties to Employers, Investment Analysis, Conflicts of Interest, Responsibilities as Members
- **Standards of Professional Conduct**: Specific behavioral requirements
- **Global Investment Performance Standards**: Standardized performance reporting

### 重要概念 | Key Concepts

**中文重点**：
- **利益冲突管理**：必须披露所有潜在冲突
- **客户利益优先**：始终将客户利益置于首位
- **独立性和客观性**：保持投资建议的独立性
- **勤勉尽责**：进行充分的尽职调查

**English Focus**:
- **Conflict Management**: Must disclose all potential conflicts
- **Client Interest First**: Always prioritize client interests
- **Independence and Objectivity**: Maintain independence in recommendations
- **Due Diligence**: Conduct thorough investigation

## 2.2 数量方法 | Quantitative Methods (8-12%)

### 时间价值 | Time Value of Money

**中文计算公式**：
- **现值公式**：PV = FV / (1 + r)^n
- **终值公式**：FV = PV × (1 + r)^n
- **年金现值**：PVA = PMT × [(1 - (1 + r)^-n) / r]
- **年金终值**：FVA = PMT × [((1 + r)^n - 1) / r]

**English Formulas**:
- **Present Value**: PV = FV / (1 + r)^n
- **Future Value**: FV = PV × (1 + r)^n
- **Annuity Present Value**: PVA = PMT × [(1 - (1 + r)^-n) / r]
- **Annuity Future Value**: FVA = PMT × [((1 + r)^n - 1) / r]

### 概率与统计 | Probability and Statistics

**中文概念**：
- **概率分布**：正态分布、二项分布、泊松分布
- **描述统计**：均值、中位数、众数、方差、标准差
- **推断统计**：假设检验、置信区间、回归分析

**English Concepts**:
- **Probability Distributions**: Normal, Binomial, Poisson distributions
- **Descriptive Statistics**: Mean, Median, Mode, Variance, Standard Deviation
- **Inferential Statistics**: Hypothesis testing, Confidence intervals, Regression analysis

### 金融计算器使用 | Financial Calculator Usage

**中文操作指南**：
- **TI BA II Plus**：时间价值计算、现金流计算、统计计算
- **HP 12C**：RPN计算方式、时间价值功能
- **常见功能**：NPV、IRR、债券计算、折旧计算

**English Guide**:
- **TI BA II Plus**: Time value, Cash flow, Statistical calculations
- **HP 12C**: RPN method, Time value functions
- **Common Functions**: NPV, IRR, Bond calculations, Depreciation

## 2.3 经济学 | Economics (8-12%)

### 微观经济学 | Microeconomics

**中文重点**：
- **供需理论**：市场均衡、价格弹性
- **消费者行为**：效用最大化、无差异曲线
- **生产者行为**：成本函数、利润最大化
- **市场结构**：完全竞争、垄断、寡头垄断

**English Focus**:
- **Supply and Demand**: Market equilibrium, Price elasticity
- **Consumer Behavior**: Utility maximization, Indifference curves
- **Producer Behavior**: Cost functions, Profit maximization
- **Market Structures**: Perfect competition, Monopoly, Oligopoly

### 宏观经济学 | Macroeconomics

**中文概念**：
- **国民收入核算**：GDP、GNP、NI的计算
- **经济增长**：生产函数、技术进步
- **经济周期**：繁荣、衰退、萧条、复苏
- **货币政策与财政政策**：央行政策、政府支出

**English Concepts**:
- **National Income Accounting**: GDP, GNP, NI calculations
- **Economic Growth**: Production functions, Technological progress
- **Business Cycles**: Boom, Recession, Depression, Recovery
- **Monetary and Fiscal Policy**: Central bank policies, Government spending

## 2.4 财务报表分析 | Financial Statement Analysis (13-17%)

### 三张主要报表 | Three Main Statements

**中文分析重点**：
- **利润表**：收入确认、费用匹配、盈利能力分析
- **资产负债表**：资产质量、负债结构、权益分析
- **现金流量表**：经营、投资、融资活动现金流

**English Analysis Focus**:
- **Income Statement**: Revenue recognition, Expense matching, Profitability analysis
- **Balance Sheet**: Asset quality, Liability structure, Equity analysis
- **Cash Flow Statement**: Operating, Investing, Financing cash flows

### 财务比率分析 | Financial Ratio Analysis

**中文比率类型**：
- **盈利能力比率**：毛利率、净利率、ROA、ROE
- **偿债能力比率**：流动比率、速动比率、负债比率
- **运营效率比率**：存货周转率、应收账款周转率
- **市场价值比率**：市盈率、市净率、股息收益率

**English Ratio Types**:
- **Profitability Ratios**: Gross margin, Net margin, ROA, ROE
- **Liquidity Ratios**: Current ratio, Quick ratio, Debt ratio
- **Efficiency Ratios**: Inventory turnover, Receivables turnover
- **Market Value Ratios**: P/E ratio, P/B ratio, Dividend yield

### 国际财务报告准则 | IFRS vs US GAAP

**中文差异**：
- **存货计价**：IFRS禁止LIFO，US GAAP允许
- **研发费用**：IFRS部分资本化，US GAAP全部费用化
- **资产重估**：IFRS允许重估增值，US GAAP不允许

**English Differences**:
- **Inventory Valuation**: IFRS prohibits LIFO, US GAAP allows
- **R&D Expenses**: IFRS partial capitalization, US GAAP full expensing
- **Asset Revaluation**: IFRS allows upward revaluation, US GAAP prohibits

## 2.5 公司金融 | Corporate Finance (8-12%)

### 资本预算 | Capital Budgeting

**中文方法**：
- **净现值法**：NPV = ∑(CFt / (1 + r)^t) - Initial Investment
- **内部收益率法**：使NPV=0的贴现率
- **投资回收期**：收回初始投资所需时间
- **盈利指数**：PV of Future Cash Flows / Initial Investment

**English Methods**:
- **Net Present Value**: NPV = ∑(CFt / (1 + r)^t) - Initial Investment
- **Internal Rate of Return**: Discount rate where NPV = 0
- **Payback Period**: Time to recover initial investment
- **Profitability Index**: PV of Future Cash Flows / Initial Investment

### 资本结构 | Capital Structure

**中文理论**：
- **MM理论**：无税和有税情况下的资本结构无关论
- **权衡理论**：债务的税盾效应与财务困境成本的权衡
- **优序融资理论**：内部融资优先，债务次之，股权最后

**English Theories**:
- **MM Theory**: Capital structure irrelevance under perfect markets
- **Trade-off Theory**: Tax shield benefits vs financial distress costs
- **Pecking Order Theory**: Internal financing first, then debt, equity last

## 2.6 权益投资 | Equity Investments (10-12%)

### 股票市场 | Stock Markets

**中文市场类型**：
- **一级市场**：新股发行市场
- **二级市场**：已发行股票交易市场
- **市场指数**：道琼斯、标普500、纳斯达克、沪深300

**English Market Types**:
- **Primary Market**: New stock issuance
- **Secondary Market**: Trading of existing stocks
- **Market Indices**: Dow Jones, S&P 500, NASDAQ, CSI 300

### 股票估值 | Equity Valuation

**中文模型**：
- **股利贴现模型**：P0 = D1 / (r - g)
- **自由现金流模型**：企业价值 = FCF / (WACC - g)
- **相对估值法**：市盈率、市净率、市销率比较

**English Models**:
- **Dividend Discount Model**: P0 = D1 / (r - g)
- **Free Cash Flow Model**: Enterprise Value = FCF / (WACC - g)
- **Relative Valuation**: P/E, P/B, P/S comparisons

## 2.7 固定收益 | Fixed Income (10-12%)

### 债券基础 | Bond Basics

**中文特性**：
- **票面利率**：债券每年支付的利息率
- **到期日**：债券本金偿还的日期
- **信用评级**：AAA、AA、A、BBB、BB等
- **收益率**：当期收益率、到期收益率、赎回收益率

**English Characteristics**:
- **Coupon Rate**: Annual interest payment rate
- **Maturity Date**: Date when principal is repaid
- **Credit Rating**: AAA, AA, A, BBB, BB, etc.
- **Yields**: Current yield, Yield to maturity, Yield to call

### 利率风险 | Interest Rate Risk

**中文度量**：
- **久期**：债券价格对利率变化的敏感度
- **凸性**：久期变化的度量，考虑价格-收益率曲线的曲率
- **基点价值**：利率变动1个基点引起的价格变化

**English Measures**:
- **Duration**: Sensitivity of bond price to interest rate changes
- **Convexity**: Measure of duration change, considers curvature
- **Basis Point Value**: Price change for 1bp interest rate change

## 2.8 衍生品 | Derivatives (5-8%)

### 期货与远期 | Futures and Forwards

**中文区别**：
- **期货**：标准化合约，交易所交易，每日结算
- **远期**：定制化合约，场外交易，到期结算
- **定价原理**：现货价格 + 持有成本

**English Differences**:
- **Futures**: Standardized, exchange-traded, daily settlement
- **Forwards**: Customized, OTC, settlement at maturity
- **Pricing**: Spot price + Cost of carry

### 期权 | Options

**中文类型**：
- **看涨期权**：有权以约定价格买入资产
- **看跌期权**：有权以约定价格卖出资产
- **内在价值**：立即行权可获得的利润
- **时间价值**：期权价格超过内在价值的部分

**English Types**:
- **Call Option**: Right to buy asset at strike price
- **Put Option**: Right to sell asset at strike price
- **Intrinsic Value**: Profit from immediate exercise
- **Time Value**: Option price minus intrinsic value

## 2.9 另类投资 | Alternative Investments (5-8%)

### 投资类型 | Investment Types

**中文类别**：
- **私募股权**：投资非上市公司
- **对冲基金**：采用多种策略的基金
- **房地产**：商业地产、住宅地产投资
- **大宗商品**：黄金、石油、农产品等

**English Categories**:
- **Private Equity**: Investment in private companies
- **Hedge Funds**: Funds using various strategies
- **Real Estate**: Commercial, residential property investment
- **Commodities**: Gold, oil, agricultural products

### 风险与回报 | Risk and Return

**中文特点**：
- **低流动性**：投资期限长，退出困难
- **高门槛**：最低投资金额要求高
- **信息不透明**：信息披露较少
- **高潜在回报**：可能获得超额回报

**English Characteristics**:
- **Low Liquidity**: Long investment horizon, difficult exit
- **High Minimums**: High minimum investment requirements
- **Information Opacity**: Limited disclosure
- **High Potential Returns**: Possibility of excess returns

## 2.10 投资组合管理 | Portfolio Management (5-8%)

### 现代投资组合理论 | Modern Portfolio Theory

**中文核心**：
- **有效前沿**：给定风险水平下的最大收益组合
- **资本市场线**：无风险资产与市场组合的连接线
- **资本资产定价模型**：E(Ri) = Rf + βi × (E(Rm) - Rf)

**English Core**:
- **Efficient Frontier**: Maximum return for given risk level
- **Capital Market Line**: Line connecting risk-free asset and market portfolio
- **CAPM**: E(Ri) = Rf + βi × (E(Rm) - Rf)

### 风险管理 | Risk Management

**中文方法**：
- **分散化**：投资不同资产降低非系统性风险
- **资产配置**：确定各类资产的投资比例
- **风险度量**：方差、标准差、在险价值

**English Methods**:
- **Diversification**: Invest in different assets to reduce unsystematic risk
- **Asset Allocation**: Determine investment percentages for different assets
- **Risk Measures**: Variance, Standard deviation, Value at Risk
