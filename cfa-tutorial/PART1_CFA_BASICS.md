# 第1部分：CFA基础概念 | Part 1: CFA Basic Concepts

## 1.1 CFA考试简介 | CFA Exam Introduction

### 什么是CFA？ | What is CFA?

**中文**：特许金融分析师（Chartered Financial Analyst，简称CFA）是全球投资业里最为严格与含金量最高的资格认证，被称为金融第一考的考试。

**English**: The Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA) designation is the most rigorous and prestigious credential in the global investment profession, often referred to as the "gold standard" in financial certifications.

### CFA历史 | CFA History

**中文**：
- 成立于1947年，由美国特许金融分析师协会（CFA Institute）设立
- 全球拥有超过190,000名持证人
- 在170多个国家和地区得到认可

**English**:
- Established in 1947 by the CFA Institute
- Over 190,000 charterholders worldwide
- Recognized in more than 170 countries and territories

### 为什么要考CFA？ | Why Pursue CFA?

**中文优势**：
- **职业认可度高**：全球金融行业金标准
- **知识体系全面**：覆盖投资管理全领域
- **职业发展广阔**：投资银行、资产管理、研究分析等
- **薪资水平提升**：持证人平均薪资显著高于行业平均水平

**English Advantages**:
- **High Professional Recognition**: Global gold standard in finance
- **Comprehensive Knowledge**: Covers entire investment management spectrum
- **Broad Career Opportunities**: Investment banking, asset management, research analysis
- **Salary Enhancement**: Charterholders earn significantly above industry average

**中文要求**：
1. **学历要求**：学士学位或同等学历
2. **工作经验**：4年相关工作经验（可在考试期间积累）

**English Requirements**:
1. **Education**: Bachelor's degree or equivalent
2. **Work Experience**: 4 years of relevant work experience (can be accumulated during exams)
3. **Language**: English reading and comprehension skills
4. **Passport**: Valid international passport

## 1.4 金融基础概念 | Basic Financial Concepts

### 时间价值 | Time Value of Money

**中文概念**：
- **现值（PV）**：未来现金流在当前的价值
- **终值（FV）**：当前投资在未来某一时间的价值
- **贴现率**：将未来价值折算为现值的利率

**English Concepts**:
- **Present Value (PV)**: Current value of future cash flows
- **Future Value (FV)**: Value of current investment at future date
- **Discount Rate**: Interest rate used to discount future values

### 风险与回报 | Risk and Return

**中文关系**：
- **高风险高回报**：风险越高，要求的回报率越高
- **系统性风险**：市场整体风险，无法通过分散化消除
- **非系统性风险**：特定公司风险，可通过分散化降低

**English Relationships**:
- **High Risk High Return**: Higher risk demands higher expected returns
- **Systematic Risk**: Market-wide risk, cannot be diversified away
- **Unsystematic Risk**: Company-specific risk, can be reduced through diversification

### 财务报表 | Financial Statements

**中文三表**：
1. **利润表**：反映企业一定时期的经营成果
2. **资产负债表**：反映企业特定时点的财务状况
3. **现金流量表**：反映企业现金流入流出情况

**English Three Statements**:
1. **Income Statement**: Shows company's financial performance over period
2. **Balance Sheet**: Shows company's financial position at specific date
3. **Cash Flow Statement**: Shows company's cash inflows and outflows

## 1.5 职业道德标准 | Ethical and Professional Standards

### 七大准则 | Seven Standards

**中文准则**：
1. **专业性**：维护专业能力和行为标准
2. **诚信正直**：保持诚实和正直
3. **对客户的责任**：将客户利益置于首位
4. **对雇主的责任**：忠诚于雇主
5. **投资分析和建议**：保持合理和独立
6. **利益冲突**：充分披露并管理冲突
7. **CFA会员责任**：维护协会声誉

**English Standards**:
1. **Professionalism**: Maintain professional competence and standards
2. **Integrity**: Act with honesty and integrity
3. **Duties to Clients**: Place client interests first
4. **Duties to Employers**: Be loyal to employers
5. **Investment Analysis and Recommendations**: Be reasonable and independent
6. **Conflicts of Interest**: Disclose and manage conflicts
7. **Responsibilities as CFA Members**: Uphold Institute's reputation
