# CFA 0基础到精通教程 | CFA Tutorial: From Beginner to Mastery

## 概述 | Overview

本教程是一个全面的CFA（特许金融分析师）学习指南，从零基础开始，逐步深入，帮助学习者系统掌握CFA三个级别的全部知识体系。教程采用中英文双语编写，适合不同语言背景的学习者。

This is a comprehensive CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) tutorial that guides learners from complete beginner to mastery level, covering all three levels of the CFA curriculum. The tutorial is written in both Chinese and English to accommodate learners from different language backgrounds.

## 考试信息 | Exam Information

### 考试时间 | Exam Dates
- **CFA一级**：2月、5月、8月、11月 | February, May, August, November
- **CFA二级**：5月、8月、11月 | May, August, November
- **CFA三级**：2月、8月 | February, August

### 考试形式 | Exam Format
- **CFA一级**：180道选择题 | 180 multiple-choice questions
- **CFA二级**：88道项目集 | 88 item sets
- **CFA三级**：构建式回答+项目集 | Constructed response + item sets

### 考试费用 | Exam Fees
- **早鸟报名**：$900-$1,250 | Early registration: $900-$1,250
- **标准报名**：$1,200-$1,450 | Standard registration: $1,200-$1,450
